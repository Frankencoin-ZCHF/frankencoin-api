Anomalies (spikes / gaps)

SPIKE — supply increases by >20% in a single step. Typical cause: a new chain's allocation suddenly added to an existing timestamp (e.g. Ponder checkpoint corruption).
GAP — supply drops by >20% in a single step. Typical cause: a forward-filled allocation disappears at a timestamp where only a subset of chains contributed data.
