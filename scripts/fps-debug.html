Realized Net Earnings (rolling 365d) & Annual Net Earnings (annualized projection)

■ realizedNetEarnings — rolling 365-day window: (totalInflow − totalOutflow) for the trailing year. Drops occur when a high-earning day rolls out of the window, or when loss events (liquidation shortfalls, bad debt) push outflow above inflow.

■ annualNetEarnings — forward projection: implied annual earnings from avg borrow rate × total minted, minus projected savings interest cost (saved amount × save rate). Forward-looking, not realized.
